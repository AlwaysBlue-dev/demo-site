About Azra Noon

Professional Psychotherapist with 10+ Years Experience

Welcome — I'm Azra Saeed Husain / Azra Noon, holding a Diploma in Humanistic Counselling from CPPD and an Advanced Diploma from CPPD. As a licensed psychotherapist, I’m passionate about supporting individuals through life’s complexities with empathy, insight, and respect.

With 10 years of clinical experience, I specialize in working with anxiety, depression, trauma recovery, relationship issues, and addictions. My approach is integrative and evidence-based, drawing from modalities such as Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT), Psychodynamic Therapy, and Mindfulness-Based Approaches, tailored to your unique needs. I continuously enhance my expertise through workshops and classes to provide comprehensive support.

Whether you're seeking help to overcome challenges or to better understand yourself, I’m here to collaborate with you in building a more balanced, meaningful life.
